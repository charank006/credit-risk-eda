# Business Insights

## Summary

- Younger applicants make up the majority of borrowers.
- Lower income levels are associated with higher loan default rates.
- Applicants with previous defaults are more likely to default again.
- Higher loan amounts and higher interest rates increase financial risk.
- Loan grade is one of the strongest indicators of repayment behavior.

## Recommendations

- Strengthen credit assessment for high-risk applicants.
- Review lending strategies for personal and medical loans.
- Consider income-to-loan ratio during approval decisions.
- Use previous default history as a key feature in risk evaluation.
